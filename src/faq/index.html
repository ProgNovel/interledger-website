- question: "●\tWhat is the Interledger Foundation’s Mission?" answer_html: >-

The Interledger Foundation is a non-profit advocate for the Interledger Protocol and the open web. We promote innovation, creativity and inclusion by advancing open payment standards and technologies that can seamlessly connect our global society. To drive our vision forward, we will connect a powerful community of web advocates and leaders; support creative and innovative solutions to connect humanity; and steward technology and standards work that advance open payment technology.
